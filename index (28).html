About ST Studio

Prague's Most

ST Studio is a sanctuary of precision and artistry nestled in the heart of Prague. Our philosophy is simple: every client deserves a bespoke experience, crafted with the same care a couturier applies to haute couture. We merge international technique with deep understanding of each individual.

From luminous balayage to architectural nail design, our atelier is where Prague's most discerning clients come to feel genuinely transformed. This is not a salon. This is a studio.

Our Specialist
Profiles

SV
Lead Stylist

Simona Vančurová

Creative Director · Hair

With 14 years in luxury salons across Prague and Vienna, Simona leads our creative vision. She trained under Frédéric Fekkai in Paris and specialises in lived-in colour fusions and precision geometric cuts. Her approach combines Continental structure with the warmth of Czech craftsmanship.

Balayage Precision Cuts Colour Theory Editorial
KM
Master Colourist

Karolína Malíková

Senior Colourist · Balayage

Karolína is a certified Wella Master Colour Expert with a background in fashion photography. She brings an artist's eye to every transformation, reading undertones and light dynamics before touching a single strand. Her signature technique — the "Prague Melt" — has earned a devoted following across Europe.
